Bakkoura Global Time Research Center

The center deals with objectification of time: research of mechanisms of time influence on human life and search for algorithms of effective time allocation.

The basis of the Center's work is sociological science and philosophical approach.

Exploration of possibilities to influence on the perception of time to make people's lives easier and happier, without stress and pressure from accelerating processes.

The center is headed by Jihad Bakkoura, the head of Bakkoura Watch House. And the work and implementation of the Center's research is based on the principles of “open code” - all data and opinions are published in open access to those who are interested in a given topic regardless of country, gender and age. And participants of the scientific and world community are invited to the discussion. Only in dialog is the truth born.

The management of the center is based on respect for the opinion of experts from different fields and countries who can influence the quality of life of people now and in the future: psychologists, logotherapists, quantum physicists, astronomers, futurologists, sociologists, etc.
